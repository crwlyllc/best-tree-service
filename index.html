Do you offer 24/7 emergency tree removal?

Yes. Call (214) 944-7415 for storm damage, leaning trees, or urgent hazards.

What factors determine the cost of tree removal?

Pricing is influenced by tree size, location, access, risk, and the complexity of the surrounding environment. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Best Tree Service DFW carries a $2 million liability policy and can provide each homeowner with a personalized certificate of insurance for their records.

Can you remove trees near power lines?

Yes. The crew coordinates with utility companies when needed and follows rigorous safety standards for tree work near power lines.

How frequently should trees be trimmed?

The ideal trimming frequency is usually every 1-3 years, depending on tree species, location, growth rate, and health. The estimator can recommend a schedule for your property.

How does the VA and military discount work?

Qualified customers can receive up to 10% off. Details are disclosed when the estimator arrives.

What payment options do you accept?

Flexible payment options include cash, check, credit cards, Venmo, and Zelle.

Do estimates include cleanup?

Yes. Cleanup is reviewed during the estimate so the scope is clear before work begins, and haul-off options are available when needed.

How deep do you grind stumps?

Standard stump grinding is typically 4-6 inches below ground level, with adjustments available based on your landscaping needs.

Do you remove roots as well?

Standard service includes grinding the stump and surface roots. More extensive root removal can be discussed during the estimate.

Can the area be replanted after stump grinding?

Yes. The area can be backfilled and prepared for sod, seed, or new plantings so it is ready for your next landscaping step.
